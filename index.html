Who We are

We are a company that freezes vegetables and fruits individually and quickly to retain their full flavour, quality and nutrients We strive to exceed customer expectations by providing excellent service and the best product at the most competitive prices. These fruits and vegetables are processed in the best way so that they can be tasted at all times of the season and we take special care to preserve their natural taste so that these foodstuffs are widely demanded in the global markets. With our advanced equipment in packaging and processing, we have proven ourselves well in the market and in our quest to strictly maintain the aspects of quality at every stage so that the fruits and vegetables provided are of better quality for customers. We strictly adhere to food industry standards for our entire variety of fruits and vegetables and operate strict quality control procedures to deliver unique fruits and vegetables to our valued customers. We have worked over years of work in which we achieved many successes and became one of the strong companies, not only in the field of export, but also in the field of investment more. We are not the ones who evaluate ourselves, but the customers who evaluate us. Where we are working to raise the status of the Egyptian product in general and Orico in particular. We deal worldwide by exporting frozen agricultural crops to Arab, African, Asian and European countries. Our products are fruits of all kinds. Orange - Mango - Guava - Dates - Romans - Grapes - Strawberry - Peach - Watermelon - Apricots Our products are vegetables of all kinds. Beans - onions - artichokes - broccoli - cauliflower - grape leaves - okra - eggplant - peas - half fried potatoes - potatoes - taro - garlic cubes - ginger cubes - molokhia - mixed vegetables - spinach - We also export all kinds of spices and spices
